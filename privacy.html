Privacy Policy

Scroll Music – © Colpham

This Privacy Policy explains how the Scroll Music app (the “App”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the application.

1. Core Principle – Your Data Is Yours

Scroll is a completely offline music player. It does not connect to the internet, does not require user accounts, and contains no third‑party analytics or advertising services. All data stays exclusively on your device.

2. Information We Access (With Your Permission)

To function, Scroll requires permission to read your device’s storage:

  • Media and Audio Files (READ_MEDIA_AUDIO / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE): This permission is essential for locating, reading, and displaying the audio files stored on your device. The data is processed only locally to build your song list, folder view, and playlists. No information is ever transmitted off the device.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

We are committed to collecting the absolute minimum data necessary. Scroll Music does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal Information – name, email address, phone number, location, or any other personal identifiers.
  • Usage Analytics – we do not track which buttons you tap, how long you listen, or your listening habits.
  • Device Information – IP address, device ID, advertising ID, or any other unique hardware/software identifiers.

4. Data Security

Because all processing occurs locally, the security of your information is primarily provided by the operating system (iOS or Android). We follow industry best practices to ensure the app itself cannot be used to gain unauthorized local access.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. Since we collect no personal data, the App is safe for all ages, though parental guidance is recommended for installations.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the “Last Updated” date will be revised accordingly. We encourage you to review the policy periodically.

Contact Us
